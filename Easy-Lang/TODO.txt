------------ Subtitle Displayer ------------ 

Subtitle Displayer DivX 5.0 is a practical and fairly simple display subtitles for DivX movies, the operation is very easy because one side is playing the film and goes next to a window displaying subtitles belonging to it.

In the author's website you can find links to several sites where you can find subtitles (extension SRT) of these DivX you've downloaded in another language and for which you need to have the subtitles to understand.
